Amina Okechukwu

Portfolio & Achievements

Design Philosophy

Amina Okechukwu believes fashion is a powerful medium for storytelling and cultural preservation. Her design philosophy centers on three core principles:

  • Heritage Reimagined: Transforming traditional African textiles and techniques for the modern wardrobe
  • Ethical Luxury: Every piece is handcrafted by skilled artisans earning fair wages in safe conditions
  • Sustainable Innovation: Using organic dyes, recycled materials, and zero-waste pattern cutting

Born in Enugu and trained at Central Saint Martins in London, Amina returned to Nigeria in 2014 to launch AMŌ Studio. Her work has been featured in Vogue, Elle, and Business of Fashion.
