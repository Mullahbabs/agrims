Frequently Asked Questions

What products does Agrim International manufacture? +

Agrim International manufactures a diverse range of products including premium beverages (Tombo Bitters, Vodka, Brandy, Whiskey, Cremica), confectionery products through Agrim Global, healthcare products through Agrim Pharmaceutical (sanitary pads, diapers, condoms, syringes), and food products through Mohan Afro Foods (Topisto Tomato).

Where are Agrim International products available? +

Our products are available in over 40 countries across Africa, the Middle East, Asia Pacific, and Europe. We are continuously expanding our distribution network to reach more markets globally.

How can I become a distributor? +

We welcome distribution partnerships. Please visit our Contact page and select "Distributor Application" in the inquiry form. Our business development team will review your application and get back to you within 5-7 business days.

Does Agrim International have sustainability initiatives? +

Yes. Sustainability is a core value at Agrim International. We have committed to achieving zero waste by 2030, implementing eco-friendly packaging solutions, reducing our carbon footprint, and supporting community development programs in the regions where we operate.

How can I apply for a job at Agrim International? +

Visit our Careers page to view current openings and submit your application. We are always looking for talented individuals to join our team across various departments and locations.
