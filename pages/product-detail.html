Product Description

Tombo Bitters is our flagship aromatic bitters, meticulously crafted using a proprietary blend of carefully selected herbs, roots, and spices sourced from around the world. Each batch is produced following traditional methods combined with modern quality control to ensure consistent, exceptional quality.

The unique formulation delivers a complex flavor profile with notes of gentian, citrus peel, cardamom, and a secret blend of African botanicals. Perfect as a digestif, cocktail ingredient, or enjoyed neat over ice.

Since its introduction, Tombo Bitters has become the preferred choice for connoisseurs and mixologists across Africa and beyond, earning numerous industry accolades for its distinctive taste and premium quality.

Usage Instructions

Serving Suggestions

  1. As a Digestif

    Pour 30-50ml into a glass. Sip neat or over ice after meals to aid digestion and enjoy the complex herbal flavors.

  2. In Cocktails

    Add 2-3 dashes to classic cocktails like Old Fashioned, Manhattan, or Negroni for enhanced depth and complexity.

  3. With Soda

    Mix 50ml Tombo Bitters with 150ml soda water or tonic over ice. Garnish with a citrus twist.

  4. Culinary Use

    A few drops can enhance sauces, marinades, and desserts with a sophisticated bitter-sweet note.

Storage Instructions

  • Temperature: Store at 15-25°C (59-77°F)
  • Light Exposure: Keep away from direct sunlight
  • Position: Store upright to prevent cork deterioration
  • Shelf Life: Indefinite when unopened and stored properly
  • After Opening: Consume within 24 months for optimal flavor
⚠️ Important Safety Information

This product contains alcohol. Please drink responsibly. Not for sale to persons under the legal drinking age. Do not consume if pregnant or nursing without consulting a healthcare professional.
