About Mohan Afro Foods

Bringing premium quality food products to African households

Authentic African Flavors, Modern Quality

Mohan Afro Foods is the food products division of Agrim International, committed to delivering premium quality food items that meet the diverse culinary needs of African families. We combine traditional food processing methods with modern technology to create products that are both authentic and of the highest quality.

Our flagship product, Topisto Tomato, has become a household name, trusted by millions of families for its rich taste and consistent quality. We source the finest tomatoes from selected farms and process them using advanced techniques to preserve natural flavor and nutrients.

Our Production Process

From farm to table - quality at every step

1

Farm Selection

We partner with carefully selected farms that grow premium tomato varieties using sustainable practices.

2

Harvesting

Tomatoes are harvested at peak ripeness to ensure maximum flavor, color, and nutritional content.

3

Sorting & Washing

Advanced sorting technology removes any imperfect tomatoes, followed by thorough washing.

4

Processing

Hot break processing at controlled temperatures to preserve natural goodness and achieve perfect consistency.

5

Concentration

Vacuum evaporation to achieve the ideal 28-30% brix concentration for rich, thick tomato paste.

6

Quality Testing

Each batch undergoes rigorous quality control testing for taste, color, consistency, and safety.

7

Packaging

Automated filling and sealing in sterile conditions to ensure product integrity and shelf life.

8

Distribution

Efficient logistics network ensuring fresh products reach markets across Africa promptly.
